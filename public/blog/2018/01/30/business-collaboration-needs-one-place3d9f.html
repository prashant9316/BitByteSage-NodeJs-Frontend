Written by Natalie | Jan 30, 2018 7:52:39 PM

Have you ever been frustrated because you can't get a hold of a colleague who is out of the office? You misplaced their cell number and just have a quick question to ask as to better make a decision? With Cisco Spark's always-on messaging, audio and video capabilities, you can message, call or video conference anywhere on any device, making it easier to get a hold of the person of the hour.

You can message one person at a time or create a space and message multiple people simultaneously. At any point if you need to take your message to a video conference, you can easily switch to video conferencing mode and then back again to messaging.

Cisco Spark is compatible with any Apple or Android device.

If you find yourself starting a phone call from your desk but need to take it on-the go, you can seamlessly move from your desk phone to your mobile device. The Cisco Spark app available for mobile devices helps keep you connected when you're away from your desk.
