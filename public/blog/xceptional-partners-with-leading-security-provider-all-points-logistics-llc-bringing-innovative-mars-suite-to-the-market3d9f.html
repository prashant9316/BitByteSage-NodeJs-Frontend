Press Release: Xceptional Partners with Leading Security Provider, All Points Logistics, LLC, Bringing Innovative MARS Suite to the Market

Written by Natalie | Sep 26, 2023 11:26:52 PM

California–Xceptional, an award-winning IT managed services provider with offices in California and Colorado, has announced a new partnership with the expert in cybersecurity, All Points Logistics, LLC, to bring innovative MARS Suite solution to the market. 

“We’re excited to enter this new partnership with All Points Logistics, LLC and bring their MARS Suite solution to the market. There is a high demand for a tool to help simplify cyber risk scoring, and MARS Suite is meeting that demand,” stated Xceptional CEO, Founder, Chris McKewon. “This solution can also help clients meet their industry compliance regulations and help optimize security posture. MARS Suite takes an overwhelming process and makes it more manageable for end-users.”

Founded in 2007 as Xceptional Networks, Inc, by Chris McKewon, the company began as a Value-Added Reseller and IT Integrator. Since that time, Xceptional has evolved and built a reputation for delivering exceptional IT managed services and security offerings that help customers drive positive business outcomes.

Established over 26 years ago as a service-disabled veteran owned small business (SDVOSB), All Points has grown into a leading solution provider to government and commercial industries supporting operations within 26 states. All Points offers software and solutions development and systems integration within mission critical industries such as space, defense, and cyber. The MARS Suite platform was created through a joint venture between All Points Logistics, LLC and Mission Multiplier - innovative providers of IT engineering, cyber security, consulting, staffing, and managed services to DoD, Federal Agencies, Armed Forces, Aerospace, Aviation, and other commercial entities. 

“Many organizations are looking for ways to reduce the complexity and cost associated with achieving and maintaining regulatory compliance, and implementing a resilient cybersecurity program,” stated Mark Dallmeier, MARS Cyber Division President, of All Points Logistics. “MARS Suite will be included within Xceptional’s portfolio of Managed Services, empowering Xceptional clients with enhanced cybersecurity, risk, and incident response capabilities.”

Those looking to learn more about All Points Logistics, LLC and Xceptional’s security solutions and service offerings can send an email to info@xceptional.com.
