Xceptional Introduces a Complete Managed IT Services Bundle on a User-Based Pricing Model

Written by justine | Dec 2, 2020 7:47:16 PM

XceptionalCare Complete gives businesses all the IT services they need at a low, monthly, user-based price.

[December 3, 2020, San Diego, CA] Xceptional, a leading Southern California-based managed IT and network technology provider, announces XceptionalCare Complete: a holistic IT management and service solution for businesses that need a full-scope managed IT provider.

While other managed IT providers typically offer device-based pricing, XceptionalCare provides full support for an organization’s entire IT environment for the low, monthly rate of $149 per user. Xceptional is the first of their local competitors to offer a comprehensive model of this kind. 

XceptionalCare Complete touches all aspects of a business’s IT, including antivirus and patching, 24/7 monitoring, asset management, and a variety of other services necessary for successful business operations. These services are provided by a bench of San Diego-based, dedicated engineers and include unlimited support tickets.

"XceptionalCare Complete is Managed IT with a security and user-experience focus. It is designed to empower users, and is equally suited to the corporate office and today’s remote workforce,” said Chris McKewon, Founder and CEO of Xceptional.

With XceptionalCare Complete, businesses no longer have to rely on expensive internal staff to monitor systems around the clock or handle trouble tickets. This comprehensive solution simplifies business IT at a predictable, low, user-based fee. Along with XceptionalCare Complete, users receive Xceptional’s unmatched customer service, and the company is looking forward to helping more businesses revolutionize their IT through care and this unique service.

Xceptional customers have commented on their experiences with the company. Julie Barnes, Partner at Jones Barnes LLC, said, “Xceptional’s hands-on approach is ideal for a small business without in-house IT expertise,” said Julie Barnes, Partner at Jones Barnes LLC.

Michael Limber, Project Manager at Secura Bio, said “Xceptional’s team impressed us with their thoroughness, attention to detail, and level of commitment. They went above and beyond, providing key solutions at every turn with a can-do attitude.”
